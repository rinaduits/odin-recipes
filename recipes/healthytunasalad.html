Healthy Tuna Salad with brown bread

Ingredients

For the Dressing:

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Cayenne Pepper (optional)
  • Lemon Zest and Juice
  • Flat Leaf Parsley

For the Tuna Salad:

  • Canned Cannellini Beans
  • Canned Tuna
  • Green Onions
  • Celery
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Capers
  • Black Olives

How to make Tuna Salad

Make the dressing:

In a small bowl, combine Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, cayenne pepper (if using), lemon zest, and lemon juice. Mix well until smooth and well-blended.

Prepare the Tuna Salad

In a large bowl, combine the drained and rinsed cannellini beans, flaked canned tuna, sliced green onions, chopped celery, halved cherry tomatoes, coarsely chopped capers, and quartered black olives. Toss gently to mix all the ingredients.

Serve and Enjoy

Divide the baby spinach among serving bowls. Spoon the tuna salad on top of the arugula or spinach, then drizzle with the prepared dressing. Alternatively, you can combine the tuna salad with the dressing and serve it on seeded bread or in a lettuce wrap for a different take.
